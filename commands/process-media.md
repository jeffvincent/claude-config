Arguments: YOUTUBE_URL DISPLAY_NAME

1. Download video from YouTube
2. Upload to Wistia (project d8417fem9e)
3. Wait for and download transcript
4. Analyze transcript and create comprehensive source document with:
   - Interview summary (2-3 paragraphs)
   - Key quotes (8-12 impactful quotes)
   - Topical breakdown with timestamps (10-15 sections)
   - Related themes
5. Identify 2-3 major themes and create/update synthesis notes
6. Delete local video file
