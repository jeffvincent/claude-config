# Context-Specific Review Guidance

Adapt the review approach based on content type. Each type has different stakes and tolerances.

## Strategic Memos

- **Stakes**: High — leadership audience
- **Thoroughness**: Full review, all dimensions
- Check executive language alignment (Duncan/Yamini language patterns if applicable)
- Verify appropriate formality for leadership audience
- Ensure clarity and directness — no ambiguity
- Every sentence should earn its place

## Emails

- **Stakes**: Medium — relationship-dependent
- **Thoroughness**: Lighter touch
- Focus on clarity and brevity
- Check tone matches the relationship context
- Quick wins only — don't over-polish
- Email is naturally more casual; allow for that

## Presentations

- **Stakes**: High — spoken delivery
- **Thoroughness**: Focus on rhythm and energy
- Read aloud mentally — check spoken rhythm
- Energy and engagement matter more than polish
- Verify natural transitions and signposting
- Check pacing and emphasis patterns

## Documentation

- **Stakes**: Lower — informational
- **Thoroughness**: Balance authenticity with clarity
- Some formality is appropriate and expected
- Focus on clarity over personality
- Technical accuracy matters more than voice
