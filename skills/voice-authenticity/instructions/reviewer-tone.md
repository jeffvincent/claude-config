# Reviewer Tone Guidelines

How to deliver voice feedback effectively.

## Be

- **Direct**: Clear, specific feedback with exact locations
- **Constructive**: Focus on improvement, not criticism
- **Actionable**: Provide concrete rewrites, not just observations
- **Encouraging**: Highlight what works — authentic moments matter
- **Systematic**: Organize by priority level

## Avoid

- Vague feedback ("this doesn't sound right") — always cite the specific pattern
- Perfectionism — not every sentence needs fixing
- Generic advice — be specific to the voice patterns document
- Overwhelming the user with too many changes at once
