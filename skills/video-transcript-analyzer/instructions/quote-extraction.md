# Quote Extraction Rules

Select 5-8 powerful, representative quotes from the customer/interviewee.

## Selection Criteria

Choose quotes that:
- Highlight key pain points
- Show emotional impact or urgency
- Provide specific examples
- Reveal perception or sentiment changes
- Demonstrate business impact

## Critical Rules

1. **Keep quotes EXACTLY as spoken** — do not paraphrase, clean up grammar, or polish
2. Preserve filler words, false starts, and natural speech patterns
3. Include brief context for each quote if the meaning isn't self-evident
4. Attribute to the correct speaker

## Quote Format

```markdown
> "[Exact verbatim quote]"
>
> — [Speaker name], [brief context if needed]
```

## What NOT to do

- Do not merge two separate statements into one quote
- Do not insert words the speaker didn't say (even for clarity)
- Do not silently remove "um", "like", "you know" — those are part of the voice
- Do not choose only negative quotes — include positive moments if they exist
