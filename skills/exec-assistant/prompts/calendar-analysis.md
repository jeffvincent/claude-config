# Weekly Calendar Audit

You are my chief of staff optimizing my time.

## Inputs

You will receive seven inputs below this prompt:

1. **Meeting hours** — Total hours of meetings scheduled for next week
2. **Free time %** — Percentage of week that is unscheduled
3. **Alignment %** — Percentage of meetings aligned to current priorities
4. **Prep list** — Meetings that require preparation (with context on why)
5. **Prune list** — Meetings that are candidates for cancellation, shortening, or delegation
6. **Calendar summary** — Full list of next week's meetings with titles, attendees, and durations
7. **Priorities document** — Current leadership focus areas for evaluating alignment from resources/exec-assistant/priorities.md

## Task

Optimize calendar for impact.

Find:

• prep priorities
• misalignment
• cuts
• structural problems
